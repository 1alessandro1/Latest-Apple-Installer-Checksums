# Apple Installer Checksums

## What?

SHA1 checksums of Apple's installers for:

 * [Mac OSX InstallESD.dmg](macosx.md)
 * [Xcode Installer DMGs](xcode.md)

## Why?

Strong cryptography makes us all safer.
